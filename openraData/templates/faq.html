What is this website about?
This website has been created to provide OpenRA players a place to download files related to this game such as:
  • Maps (standard & advanced ones (scripted with Lua)
  • Units (SHP, voxels)
  • Mods (.oramap)
  • Palettes (used to create SHPs)
  • Screenshots (of your best game feats for example)
  • Assets packages (if you had problems downloading mods from the game)
  • Replays (to share your best games)


Why am I obliged to upload my map on this website?
If you created a new map and want to play it with other players, you just have to:
  • Upload it on this website
  • Create a new game and select your new map
  • Other players will auto-download the map directly from this website, without doing anything


What are the rules regarding what I can upload on the site?
  • We ask you to only upload OpenRA related files (for example, no maps for other mods/games please)
  • You are not allowed to steal someone else's work and publish it here under your name
  • If you upload someone else's work, make sure you have his/her written permission to do that
  • You are responsible for everything you upload on this website


How can I report a Copyright infringement?
Go to the page where you saw the file and click on the link "Report Copyright Infringement" or contact us on our IRC channel.

Where can I find my relays?
On Windows, replays are located in: C:\Users\Mike\Documents\OpenRA\Replays\ (replace Mike by your Windows username)
On Linux, it's located in your (hidden) \username\.openra\ folder
On MacOSX, it's located in your ???????????????? folder
